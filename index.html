Weiyun Jiang

I'm a PhD student at Rice University, where I work on imaging through turbulence. My PhD advisor is Ashok Veeraraghavan. I also collaborate closely with Chris Metzler. In 2024, I worked as a research intern at the Mobile Processor Innovation Lab at Samsung Research America.

I have an MS in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University, where I worked in Gordon Wetzstein's lab. I have a BS in Electrical Engineering from UC Santa Barbara, where I worked in Zheng Zhang's lab.
